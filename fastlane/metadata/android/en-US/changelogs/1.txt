First release of Wallora.

• Multi-source wallpaper browsing — Pexels, Wallhaven, Unsplash, and Reddit simultaneously
• Portrait-only filtering — only wallpapers that fit a phone screen
• Auto-change wallpaper — scheduled rotation with configurable interval and time-of-day slots
• Live wallpaper & parallax — set Wallora as a live wallpaper for a depth effect
• Favourites — long-press any wallpaper to save; browse your collection offline
• Category browsing — curated topics (Nature, Minimal, Dark/AMOLED, Space, Architecture, and more)
• Custom topics — add your own search keywords as browsable categories
• Reddit subreddits — toggle from pre-configured subreddits or add your own
• Quick Settings tile — change wallpaper from the notification shade
• Home-screen widget — current wallpaper thumbnail + one-tap "Next" button
• Material You — dynamic colour extracted from the live wallpaper bitmap
